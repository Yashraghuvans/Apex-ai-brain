# Code of Conduct

## Our Commitment

Apex AI Brain is committed to providing a welcoming and inclusive environment for all contributors and community members. We are dedicated to creating a safe space free from harassment, discrimination, and disrespect.

## Our Standards

Examples of behavior that contributes to creating a positive environment include:

- Being respectful and inclusive of differing opinions, experiences, and backgrounds
- Giving and receiving constructive feedback gracefully
- Focusing on what is best for the community
- Showing empathy towards other community members
- Using welcoming and inclusive language
- Helping others learn and grow

Examples of unacceptable behavior include:

- Harassment, intimidation, or bullying (online or offline)
- Discriminatory jokes or comments related to race, gender, sexual orientation, religion, disability, appearance, or age
- Personal attacks or insulting remarks
- Publishing private information without consent
- Trolling or spamming
- Sexual or violent content
- Any other conduct that is clearly inappropriate

## Enforcement

Violations of this Code of Conduct should be reported to the maintainers at the earliest opportunity. All reports will be reviewed and investigated promptly and confidentially.

Community members who violate this Code of Conduct may face:
- Verbal warning and explanation of the issue
- Temporary muting or comment removal
- Permanent removal from the community

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies to all community spaces including:
- GitHub issues and pull requests
- Email communications
- Community discussions
- Project forums or chat channels
